2024 Trend Predictions

 

Metallics

We're going to see a lot more metallics this year. We will see them in a wide range of different products from shoes, bags, to actual clothing items like skirts. This trend is carrying over from the end of 2023.

 

Bows

Bows are a more recent trend that will be following over from 2023. Strongly aligned with the 'croquet' trend, bows are going to be seen anywhere and everywhere. Styled on clothes, in hair, on furniture, we're going to see them taking over.

 

Exposed Socks

A trend we have seen before but is being brought back in a different way. Exposed socks and leg warmers are being brought back in a slightly more feminine way. Cutely decoracted with tennis shoes, and atheletic socks with ballet flats, there is a wide range of ways to style this trend.
